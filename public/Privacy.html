Privacy


Minimo respects the right of users to be informed of the collection of their personal data and other transactions using such information. When using data that can directly or indirectly identify you, we use the principle of strict necessity. For this reason, the use of your personal data has been minimized and consistent with the purposes for which your personal data is collected and / or processed. Client - an individual or legal entity that carries out the Order and buys the Goods presented on the website www.amf.com.ua. The Client’s account (or Personal Account) is his account where personal information about the client is stored, including login, password, full name, e-mail, contact numbers and addresses, information about the status of orders, etc. ) Personal data is collected, processed and stored by Minimo, solely for the purposes of providing services for the sale of goods and fulfilling its obligations to customers who purchase Minimo products. By clicking on the “Sign up for our newsletter!”, “Register” button, you confirm the correctness of the data you filled in and agree to the transfer of the data to Minimo employees for your further service. Your personal data is not transferred to third parties for purposes not permitted by law, and without your consent. Minimo also restricts access to third parties and company employees who are not involved in placing your order or your service. Your personal data may be transferred to third parties only if it is necessary to process the order for your purposes. For example, to deliver the goods you have chosen by courier, we inform them of the required place of unloading, the contact person of the recipient and the list of goods you ordered to be delivered. Please inform about any changes in your personal data by e-mail info@amf.com.ua or directly to your manager to ensure that your personal data is always up to date.
